Thank you so much for your participation! This study is investigating the role of cognitive flexibility in the healing power of psychedelics. One of the current theories behind the effectiveness of psychedelics therapies is that they allow people to be more cognitively flexible. As a result, they are able to reframe or let go of maladaptive narratives and beliefs. The different parts of this study tested different ways someone might be cognitively flexible. The hope is that ultimately these insights can help inform treatment for clinical populations.

If you know of anyone else who has recently used psychedelics, and especially if they have recently been on a retreat, please feel free to forward them information about participating in this study.
